About Negin

Negin is a Vancouver-based multidisciplinary creative with a foundation in Fine Art, Theater (Dramatic Literature), and Journalism. Her diverse educational background gives her work a unique voice deeply expressive, richly layered, and grounded in storytelling.

Her journey began on the stage and in the newsroom, where she explored human experiences through words and performance. She later earned her Master's degree in Fine Art, developing a passion for portrait painting and the emotional power of visual expression. Journalism sharpened her sense of clarity, structure, and audience skills that now infuse her graphic design work.

Currently studying Graphic Design at BCIT, Negin blends her creative roots with practical design tools to build compelling brand identities, magazine layouts, and marketing pieces that feel both artistic and strategic.

Creative Philosophy

She believes design is more than decoration—it's a form of communication that must be intentional, emotional, and well-crafted. Her artistic side brings soul; her journalistic side brings structure.
